About Tao Records

Turning Passion into Harmony: The Tao Records Story

Our journey began in a cozy downtown cafe, where a diverse group of friends united over a shared dream: to create music that inspires souls.

We weren't industry insiders—just everyday people with an unwavering passion for melody. Armed with enthusiasm and creativity, we faced rejections and challenges, but our resolve only grew stronger.

When we shared our music on YouTube, something magical happened. Our heartfelt creations resonated with people worldwide, transcending boundaries and connecting lives.

Today, Tao Records has grown beyond our wildest dreams. We've partnered with over 25 music industry leaders, managed nearly 100 YouTube channels, and amassed an incredible 100 million views. Yet, our core remains unchanged. We're still those friends making music in a cafe, only now, we've invited the world to our jam session.

Our mission are to create inspiring music, tell meaningful stories, and show that with passion and perseverance, ordinary people can create extraordinary harmony.

At Tao Records, we don't just make music. We make possibilities. Join us in our world of sound, where every note carries a dream.
